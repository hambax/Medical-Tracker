How it helped our family

When my dad was living with a brain tumour, care became a stream of medication timing, nausea, headaches, appetite, confusion, energy, and quiet changes that were easy to miss. This tracker helped us bring clearer notes into conversations with clinicians and care with more confidence until his passing.

It also helped share the load. Family, caregivers, and the patient could take shifts without starting from scratch: one glance showed which medicines had been taken, what was still coming up, and whether anything looked missed.
